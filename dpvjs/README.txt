              INSTRUCTIONS  
                                    
ENTER - Launch
Hold ESC - Exit

Q - Figure Width & OVER2 Warp X frequency
W - Figure Opacity & OVER2 Warp Y frequency
E - Figure Sine magnitude & OVER2 Warp X amplitude
R - Figure Sine period & OVER2 Warp Y amplitude
T - Figure rotate speed & OVER2 Warp X speed & BG angle
Y - BG Warp X frequency
U - BG Warp Y frequency & OVER2 Warp Y speed
I - BG Warp X amplitude & OVER2 Glow Vertical
O - BG Warp Y amplitude & OVER2 Glow Horizontal
P - BG Warp Ripple Frequency
A - BG Inversion & BG Warp Ripple Amplitude
S - BG Radial Pixellate & BG Warp Ripple Speed
D - BG Warp X speed & OVER1 Warp X amplitude
F - BG Warp Y speed & OVER1 angle
G - OVER1 Warp X amplitude
H - OVER1 Warp X speed & OVER2 angle
J - OVER1 Warp Y speed
K - OVER1 Warp X frequency
L - OVER1 Warp Y frequency

Z - Maximum BG contrast
X - Change OVER1 color
C - Change OVER2 color
V - Set OVER1 and OVER2 color to 0
B - OVER1 opacity
N - OVER2  opacity
M - Max everything

Up Arrow - Blink white
Down Arrow - Blink black

SPACE - Random color scheme (No BG change)
SHIFT - Empty preset
CTRL - No color scheme
ENTER - Random preset

. - Normal speed
, - Slow speed
` - Pause

1 - Information about preset
